About Us

The CAD/CAM Study Center at the University of Holguín is the first and only center of its kind within Cuba's Ministry of Higher Education. Founded in 1985 as a research group, it became an official center in 1989.

Our initial mission was the development of mechanical engineering software for clients in national and international markets, including Colombia, Mexico, Spain, and Switzerland.

Today, we continue to be pioneers in engineering research and education, with a focus on CAD/CAM/CAE technologies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality undergraduate and postgraduate education and to apply our research to solving scientific-technical problems, all through a strong university-community partnership and a team of experts with solid scientific training in CAD/CAM/CAE technologies.
